Our Process

From project planning to final reporting, we follow a systematic and quality-driven workflow.

01

Requirement Discussion

Understanding client objectives, project scope, and expected outputs.

02

Project Planning

Developing survey methodology, sampling strategy, and implementation plan.

03

Enumerator Training

Comprehensive training on questionnaires, digital tools, and field protocols.

04

Field Data Collection

Nationwide data collection using SurveyCTO, ODK, Kobo, and GIS-enabled devices.

05

Data Processing

Data cleaning, validation, statistical analysis, and visualization.

06

Reporting & Delivery

Preparing professional reports, dashboards, presentations, and recommendations.

WHAT WE DO

DMA is a pioneering organization serving globally in Organizational Capacity Building and Research and Evaluation areas. We foster growth through inclusive, need-based solutions for diverse client needs.

ORGANIZATION AND INSTITUTION DEVELOPMENT

DMA takes Organizational Development and Change Management approach to institutional development and capacity building. Our approach is based on appreciative methodologies and customized with client organizations’ needs. We accompany organizations in capacity building planning and provide follow-up through support systems.

RESEARCH AND EVALUATION

DMA is a trusted partner in high-impact research, evaluation, and thematic studies across diverse development sectors. We specialize in baseline and endline evaluations, field research, and technical support in thematic areas such as Climate Change, Governance, Economic Growth, Health, Nutrition, and Human Rights.

Sectoral Presence

Our strong team of specialist consultants, field investigators, and data management professionals are experienced in all facets of the research process—from design to implementation and dissemination.
