Frequently Asked Questions

What happens after I join the Talent Pool?

Our HR and data management teams regularly review the talent pool database. When a new research or field project matches your skill set, you will be fast-tracked for evaluation and interviews.

Are the data collector positions remote or field-based?

Data Collector and Field Supervisor positions typically require extensive field travel across various districts in Bangladesh for real-time survey operations and data collection.

Can I apply for multiple positions?

Yes, you can apply for more than one position if your background and expertise align with the job requirements. Please submit separate profiles for each role.
